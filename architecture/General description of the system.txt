Description
"CARDS" is a card game for one or two players.

Game rules:
The order in which the game starts is determined randomly.
At the beginning of the game, each player receives 7 random cards.
The cards are divided into 5 types: Ninja, Wizard, Archer, Knight, Bishop.
The cards are divided into 4 rarities: Legendary, Epic, Rare, Basic.
During a turn, the player can choose several options.
First, Player can attack opponent's card. For this option player must know attacker's index and opponent's card index. 
Second, Player can combine own cards with the same types and rarities. For this option player must know two indexes of his cards. The rarity of the combined card increases.
Fhird, Player can use super power if his mana allows.
Forth, show score of the players.
Fifth, save game.
Sixth, exit from game.
After each attack, the card's mana increases.
There are three super powers in game: Freeze, Fire, Storm.
Player gets score points for destroying enemy's cards and for combine own cards.
After the end of the turn, the turn goes to the opponent.
The game continues until one of the player runs out of cards.
The player who has the remaining cards wins and destroy.
At the end of the game, player's score points appear.

Tips:
1. It's not always best to combine cards for a winning strategy.
2. It is best to attack the opponent's cards with the highest card strength.
3. When mana reaches a certain value, it no longer accumulates.
4. If you enter the card indexes incorrectly, your code is skipped.
